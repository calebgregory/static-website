What: Picnic, food made over a fire, maybe some music
Where: Edwin Warner Picnic Shelter
When: This Sunday evening, at 6

Here's a picture of Shelter 8:

And here are directions to it: http://maps.nashville.gov/NashvilleParksFinder/default.htm?parkFeatureID=44

Make sure you give that map a good look before leaving. Shelter 8 isn't on Google Maps, but Shelter 9 is, so you can use that to get there, and then stop at Shelter 8. Look on the maps.nashville.gov map to see how Shelter 8 and 9 relate to one another geographically.

There's a chimney at this picnic shelter. I'm going to stack stones and cook over the fire that way. If you have camping pots or pans, please bring them. If you have a camping grill or stove, that'd be great to bring, too. We can divvy up preparation responsibilities there. The prep is part of the fun :).

In the way of food, I'm going to bring and prepare:

  • Black beans
  • Avocados
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Red bell peppers
  • Mushrooms
  • Brussel sprouts
  • Couscous (maybe)

Feel free to invite friends, too! The more, the merrier. Just let me know so I can make sure we have enough food.

If you think it'd be interesting, you can bring a musical instrument, toys, ideas, or anything you enjoy and think would be cool to share.

This particular picnic shelter cost $30 to reserve. I'm ok with taking on the cost this time around, but if you want to send me some money, I definitely won't say no. In the future I'd like to split up the cost of reservation, and I'm curious about what it looks like just to show up at places without making a reservation.
